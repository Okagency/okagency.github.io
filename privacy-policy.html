Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 4, 2026

1. Overview

TubeDeck ("the App") is a YouTube subscription management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information through Google Sign-In and YouTube API:

  • Google Account Information: Your email address and display name for authentication purposes.
  • YouTube Data: Your YouTube subscription list, channel information, and video metadata (read-only access via youtube.readonly scope).

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Displaying your YouTube subscriptions within the App
  • Organizing channels into custom collections and folders
  • Showing latest videos from your subscribed channels
  • Managing bookmarked videos

4. Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device. The App does not transmit or store your personal data on any external server. Authentication tokens are stored securely using encrypted storage on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For user authentication
  • YouTube Data API v3: For accessing YouTube subscription and video data (read-only)

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data remains on your device.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Signing out of the App
  • Clearing the App data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
