Privacy Policy - Cubby Explorer

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Cubby Explorer ("we", "our", "the app") is a file manager application for Android. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

Cubby Explorer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device.

3. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions to function properly:

  • Storage Access - To browse, manage, copy, move, and delete files on your device.
  • Internet / Network - To connect to SMB, FTP, SFTP servers, and cloud services (OneDrive, Dropbox) that you configure.
  • Biometric - To provide app lock functionality using fingerprint authentication. Biometric data is processed by the Android system and never accessed by the app.
  • Notifications - To show progress of file operations running in the background.

4. Cloud Services

When you connect to third-party cloud services (OneDrive, Dropbox), you authenticate directly with those services using their official OAuth flows. We do not store or have access to your cloud service passwords. Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device only.

5. Network Connections

When you connect to network file servers (SMB, FTP, SFTP), the connection credentials you enter are stored locally on your device. They are never transmitted to us or any third party.

6. Data Storage

All app data, including bookmarks, settings, server credentials, and cached file information, is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers operated by us.

7. Third-Party Services

The app integrates with the following third-party services at your discretion:

  • Microsoft OneDrive - Subject to Microsoft's Privacy Policy
  • Dropbox - Subject to Dropbox's Privacy Policy
  • Google Cast - Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
